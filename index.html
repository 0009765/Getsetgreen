About GetSetGreen Podcast

GetSetGreen is a podcast dedicated to inspiring sustainable living through stories of changemakers, innovative solutions, and practical tips for reducing your environmental impact.

Meet Your Host: Pooja R V

I’m Pooja, an environmental enthusiast passionate about empowering individuals and communities to make greener choices. With GetSetGreen, I bring you engaging conversations with experts, activists, and innovators.

Why Listen?

  • Learn about circular fashion, renewable energy, and climate action.
  • Discover actionable eco-solutions you can implement right away.
  • Stay motivated with inspiring stories from real changemakers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to equip you with knowledge and inspiration to live sustainably and help create a healthier planet for future generations.
